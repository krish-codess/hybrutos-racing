Who We Are

Pushing Boundaries Since Day One

Hybrutos Racing is a student-led formula racing team dedicated to designing, building, and racing high-performance hybrid electric vehicles that compete at the highest level.

We are a multidisciplinary team of engineers, designers, and racing enthusiasts united by our passion for automotive innovation. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge hybrid powertrains that combine the raw power of combustion engines with the efficiency and instant torque of electric motors.

Every component of our vehicles is meticulously designed, simulated, and tested to achieve optimal performance while maintaining reliability and safety. We compete in Formula Student competitions worldwide, constantly refining our approach and pushing the limits of what's possible.

Our Technology

Innovation in Every Component

Hybrid Powertrain

Seamlessly integrated combustion and electric motors delivering 450+ combined horsepower with intelligent power distribution.

  • Dual-source power management
  • Regenerative braking system
  • Optimized energy recovery

Advanced Aerodynamics

CFD-optimized bodywork and active aerodynamic elements that adapt to driving conditions for maximum downforce and efficiency.

  • Computational fluid dynamics
  • Active rear wing system
  • Optimized cooling ducts

Carbon Fiber Monocoque

Lightweight, ultra-rigid chassis constructed from aerospace-grade carbon fiber composites for superior strength-to-weight ratio.

  • Autoclave-cured construction
  • FEA-validated structure
  • Integrated safety cell

Telemetry & Data

Real-time vehicle monitoring with 200+ sensors streaming data for live analysis and post-race optimization.

  • Multi-channel data acquisition
  • Wireless telemetry system
  • AI-powered analytics

Suspension Geometry

Double wishbone setup with adjustable dampers and anti-roll bars for precise handling characteristics.

  • Pushrod-actuated system
  • Four-way adjustable shocks
  • Kinematic optimization

Battery Technology

High-density lithium-ion battery pack with advanced thermal management and fast-charging capabilities.

  • 400V electrical architecture
  • Liquid cooling system
  • Custom BMS integration
